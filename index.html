Projects

Android Pet App

This is a group project that I worked on last spring in my Data Structures and Algorithms class. We built the app in Android Studio. The idea behind the app is to provide a place for people to organize important information about their pets to be useful in everyday life and to also serve as a sort of scrapbook for looking back on past pets. We made it so that the user can store biological information, care instructions, and images for their pets and it lets them schedule and view appointments. Information and pictures that the user adds are stored in Google’s Firebase Realtime database. This ensures that when the user closes the app and reopens it, their data from the previous session will have been preserved.
